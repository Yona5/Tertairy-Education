Tertiary Education in Mauritius

The Tertiary education is maintained by the Ministry of Tertiary Education, Science, Research and Technology. The Tertiary Education Commission has the responsibility for allocating public funds and planning and coordinating the development of post-secondary education. Its strategic plan envisages Mauritius as a regional knowledge hub and a centre for higher learning and excellence.
